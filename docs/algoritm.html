The algorithm

The story of our algorithm begun with the search for a question (or several) that could be asked and answered based on our dataset. As library-lovers, we chose to think of the needs and curiosities of the persons using the library and ended up with these questions:

  • Does the dataset contain a specific book?
  • Does the dataset contain books by an author?
  • Is a certain book popular or not?
  • Which are the most popular books within a shelf (genre)?
  • Which are the top ten most popular shelves (genres)?
