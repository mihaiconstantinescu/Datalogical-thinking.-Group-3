So who did what?

While most of the work in this process was done collaboratively, each person had a special area that they were responsible for. We worked in iterations and met up every couple of days to check each others work and work together before continuing on the next section.

Björn

Björn started the work of developing the API requests and the pseudocode, making sure that we had an idea of what we wanted to achieve with the algoritmhs.

Emma

Emma took the lead on finding and preparing our dataset. She also created the csv-file and made sure that the data was clean and ready to go. Also Zoom-master.

Mihai

Mihai was our GitHub wizard. Mihai also took lead on filling and structuring the website content, making sure everything is where it needs to be.

Sofia

Sofia was in charge of the time plan and led the work on the website where we present our project. Other than that she jumped in to helped where needed.

Because none of us had previous experience related to the assignment, we chose areas randomly, where each person was responsible for making sure that tasks in their area was planned and executed. As previously mentioned, we have all worked on all parts of the assignment.
