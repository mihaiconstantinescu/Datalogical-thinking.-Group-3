Welcome to the house of algorithmic horrors!

(to be pronounced with the voice of Vincent Price)

In honor of halloween... Tales of high adventure on the dark seas of datasets await the weary traveller who dares to step inside the chambers of this haunted mansion. The first chamber (aka homepage) tells only the beginning of the story and offers glimpses of what awaits in the rest of the house. The second chamber (aka the Algorithm page) takes you inside the maze of the algorithm from which only the cunning escape! The third chamber (Dataset page) boils down a library to the most basic information in the format of a csv file.

Those that dare enter the last chamber will have to face the final Bosses: the evil masterminds that created this soulless house of ghosts! (please insert in your mind the image of Scooby-Doo saying ghosts and being scared).

For serious purposes: This website was created as part of a group project for the Datalogical Thinking course of the Master’s in Information Science: Digital Environments, Borås University. The basic idea of the project was to curate a dataset, design an algorithm based on the dataset and publish everything on GitHub.
