The algorithm

The story of our algorithm begun with the search for a question (or several) that could be asked and answered based on our dataset. As library-lovers, we chose to think of the needs and curiosities of the persons using the library and ended up with these questions:

  • Does the dataset contain a specific book?
  • Does the dataset contain books by an author?
  • Is a certain book popular or not?
  • Which are the most popular books within a shelf?
  • Which are the top ten most popular shelves?

The algorithm was design with modern library clients needs in mind; such as author search, book search as well as find new material to read based on popularity. We decided to put the limit for popularity at a value of 30, but it's an arbitrary number that could easily be changed. We used George Polya's method (Dale & Lewis, 2020) as a problem solving method, iteratively working through the decisions.
